About Paramount

Paramount Supply Chain Management & Contracting is a privately held business that was founded on January 2019 by Hawraz Jamal in Iraq, Slemani. Paramount Co is a sister-company of Sheraz Int. Trade & Transport, who is our backbone logistics support. Paramount Co focuses on construction services & supply chain management services such as End-To-End logistics, local & International Procurement Services, Warehousing, Inventory Management, Tendering, and along with many other Value-Added Management Services covering whole of Iraq.

Paramount Co consists of two main departments and they are Supply Chain Management & Contracting. SCM is one the trending business models which involves procurement, logistics, warehousing, and tendering the contracts related to any specific transaction. The business strategy is a success because it allows you to manage these important business departments all together. Technology has allowed businesses to manage their supply chain more efficiently, and it has made them more profitable and at the same time more customer centric. Therefore, it is inevitable and soon, every company needs digitize their business in order to compete in the ecosystem.

The second part is Contracting, which mainly involves on site construction project management. Our Strategy at Paramount is to link on site management with the SCM department, in order to manage & deliver any turnkey projects successfully.

With our experience in logistics and supply chain management from working with our sister company, we’re not only experts in distribution logistics but also merchandising, procurement, manufacturing, and global logistics and from a strategic, tactical, and operational perspective. This means that we can truly deliver the best fit supply chains.
