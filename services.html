Supply Chain Management

Supply chain management: is the management of the flow of goods and services, between businesses and locations, and includes the movement and storage of raw materials, work-in-process inventory, and finished goods as well as end-to-end order fulfillment from point of origin to point of consumption. We at Paramount focus on supply chain management through our services.


As part of supply chain management service, we supply the following: A distribution assessment: where we analyse the impact of key variables A voice of customer assessment: where we probe target customers on various parameters to help shape supply chain requirements in the context of the client offering, CRM approach, competitive vulnerability, and extended service offerings. Best practice benchmarking: against best-in-class Supply-Chain Operations Reference-model (SCOR) methodologies to develop strengths and weaknesses.


Adapting the original concept of supply chain management, our goal is to digitize our services by adding a special digital system that in the future not only big factories will benefit from it, but it will benefit other businesses as well. Adding the door-to-door service to the supply chain management, we will work with them in every step from tendering to warehousing.
